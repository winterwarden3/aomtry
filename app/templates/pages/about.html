About Us

Our Story

Adarsh Oil Mill was founded in 2062 BS (2005 AD) by Jivanath Baral with a vision to provide pure, natural, and high-quality products to every household in Nepal.

What began as a small traditional oil press in Mainapokhar, Bardiya, has grown into a trusted local brand known for authenticity, purity, and customer trust.

Today we proudly supply premium mustard oil, rice, flour, turmeric, and natural by-products to families across the region.
